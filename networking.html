Career Fair Prep

Career fairs are great places to start networking. Whether your goal is to connect with companies that are hiring, or simply practice your elevator pitch, the career fair is the place to get started.

What to bring to the Career Fair:

  1. Resume: Printed and prepared to desomstrate your skills.
  2. Elevator Pitch: Think about what makes you unique, authentic, and worthwhile.
  3. List of Employers: Know who you want to talk to and what questions to ask.
  4. Yourself: Get some rest the night before and be ready to present your best self.

Check out the UMSI Career Fair Prep handbook to learn more and begin preparing.

Need feedback? You can always meet with a CDO Career Coach to review your materials.
